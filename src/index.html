Welcome to Painting Mathematics Library


Instructions


Currently companies that stripe new paved or seal coated parking lots do all of their estimates by hand. This library is made to do calculations for anyone building an application to create estimates for line painting. The only measurements that the estimator needs is the length of the lot, and the length of the line to the end cap. When these two numbers are entered into "paintMath" formulas it will calculate all the measurements for different types of lot lines.


PaintMath Formulas

Key:

LotLength: The length of the lot where the stalls are going to be painted.

EndCapLength: The length of the line from one end to the other.
